The writing system of Cantonese uses traditional Chinese characters. For typos caused by [merged simplified characters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ambiguities_in_Chinese_character_simplification), please refer to [簡化合併字表](http://www.kaom.net/font_jianhua_hbz.php) and [簡化同形字表](http://www.kaom.net/font_jianhua_txz.php).

## Common typos and recommended orthography

{{< alert title="Note">}}
Some morphemes have more than one recommended characters, which means either character is fine. This is out of the considereation of regional differences in writing traditions.
{{< /alert >}}

| Jyutping              | Characters | Explanations & Criteria                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| --------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| bei2                  | 比畀俾     | All pronounced bei2，while<ul><li> 比 means "compare"</li><li> 畀 means "give", "passive voice marker".</li><li>俾 is a rare word, mostly used for translations or proper nouns such as 俾斯麥.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| dei2/dei6             | 地哋       | Both have the pronunciation of **dei6** and:<ul><li>哋 can only by used in plural pronouns such as 我哋 and 佢哋.</li><li>地 means "ground". "place" etc. It has another pronunciation **dei2** for adjectives, e.g. 神神地.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| di1/dit1              | Dd 滴尐啲  | All pronounced di1 or dit1. If meaning "a tiny amount", the character should be 啲, the rest characters are deprecated.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| duk1                  | 篤督㞘㧻涿 | All pronunced **duk1**, among which<ul><li>㞘 means "the end", "tailing". E.g. 行到㞘.</li><li>㧻 is a verb, meaning "poke", "prod".</li><li>涿 is a classifier, as in 一涿屎, 一涿尿.</li><li>督 means "supervise", "monitor" as in 監督.</li><li>篤 means "firmly believe" as in 篤信, 篤定.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                  |
| fan3                  | 瞓訓       | All pronounced **fan3**, among which<ul><li>瞓 means "sleep", e.g. 瞓覺.</li><li>訓 means "training" as in 訓練, 訓教 etc.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| faan1                 | 翻返番     | Each can be pronounced **faan1**, among which<ul><li>翻 means "across", "climb"</li><li> 返 means "return", "go to" or aspect marker. E.g. 返工, 整返好佢. It can also be pronounced as **faan2**.</li><li>番 means "times" or "foreign".</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| gam2/gam3             | 噉咁       | Both have the meaning of "such", "so", among which<ul></li><li>噉 is pronounced **gam2**, it is a pronoun as in 噉樣嘅話.</li><li>咁 is an adverb, meaning "so" as in 咁犀利, and is pronounced **gam3**.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| gau6                  | 舊嚿       | Both are pronounced **gau6**<ul><li>嚿 is a classifier, meaning "chunk", "piece".</li><li> 舊 means "old".</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| ge3                   | 既嘅噶     | <ul><li>嘅 is pronounced **ge3**, posessive marker. E.g. 我嘅書</li><li> 既 is pronounced **gei3**. E.g. 既然.</li><li> 噶 is pronounced **got3**, onomatopoeia.</ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| go2/go3               | 嗰個果     | <ul><li>嗰 is a determiner, pronounced **go2** E.g. 嗰個人 meaning "that person".</li><li>個 is a classifier, pronounced **go3**. E.g. 一個人.</li><li>果 is pronounced gwo2, meaning "fruit".</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| haa2/haa5             | 吓下       | <ul><li>吓 is a modal, pronounced **haa2**, E.g. "吓？係真嘎？", "係噉嘅咩吓？".</li><li>下 means "down" when pronounced **haa6**, e.g. 下面. When pronoucned **haa5** it means "times", e.g. 畀我睇下, 試下先.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| hai6                  | 係系喺繫   | <ul><li>係 is a copula, pronounced **hai6**</li><li> 系 is pronounced **hai6**, meaning "series" e.g. 系列</li><li>喺 is a preposition, pronounced **hai2**, meaning "at", "in", as in 我喺度.</li><li>繫 is pronounced **hai6**, meaning "contact", "connect".</li> </ul>                                                                                                                                                                                |
| je5                   | 野嘢       | Both are pronounced **je5**, while:<ul><li>嘢 is a noun, meaning "things".</li><li>野 is a adjective, e.g. 野外.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| lai4/lei4/loi4        | 來嚟黎     | <ul><li> 來 is pronounced **loi4**, e.g. 將來, 未來.</li><li>嚟 is pronounced **lai4** or **lei4**, meaning "come", e.g. 過嚟.</li><li>黎 is pronounced **lai4**, E.g. 黎明 .                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| mou4/mou5             | 無冇       | Both are negation words, among which<ul><li>無 is pronounced **mou4**.</li><li>冇 is pronounced **mou5** .                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| nam2/lam3             | 諗惗冧     | <ul><li>諗 is pronounced **nam2**, meaning "think"</li><li>惗 is pronounced as **nam2**, a character variant of 諗, its usage is deprecated.</li><li>冧 is pronounced **lam3**, meaning "collapse", e.g. 冧屋, 冧樓.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| sai2                  | 使駛洗     | All pronounced **sai2**, while:<ul><li>使 means "use" as in 使用, 使錢, 唔使客氣. It has another pronounciation **si3** which means "mission", "ambassy", e.g. 使者, 信使, 大使館.</li><li>駛 means "drive", e.g. 駕駛.</li><li>洗 means "clean", "wash", e.g. 洗衫.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                            |
| saai3                 | 曬晒嗮哂   | <ul><li>曬 is pronounced **saai3**, means "to bask in the sun", e.g. 曬太陽.</li><li>晒 is pronounced "saai3", it is an adverb and an aspect marker. E.g. 食晒飯.</li><li>嗮 is a character variant of "晒" also pronounced **saai3**, its usage is deprecated.</li><li>哂 is pronounced **can2**, meaning "tease", "ridicule", e.g. 哂笑.</li></ul>                                                                                                      |
| zo2                   | 左唨咗     | all pronounced **zo2**, among which<ul><li>左 means direction "left", e.g. 左手.</li><li>咗 is a perfective aspect marker, e.g. 食咗飯.</li><li>唨 is a variant of 咗, its usage is deprecated.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| zyu3/zyu6/zoek3/zoek6 | 著住着     | <ul><li>著 is pronounced **zyu3**, e.g. 著作, 著名.</li><li>住 is pronounced **zyu6**, it can be an auxillary verb such as 袋住先. It can also be a verb meaning "live", e.g. 居住.</li><li>着 has two pronounciations, when pronounced **zoek3** it is a verb, meaning "wear" as in 着衫. When pronounced **zoek6** it means "start", "begin", e.g.着火, 着色. Note that it can also be a continuous aspect marker in Mandarin, e.g. "唱着歌".</li></ul> |
